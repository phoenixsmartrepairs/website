Phoenix Smart Repairs is a fully mobile car body repair service. We come to your home, workplace, or anywhere convenient — so you don't need to take time off work or sit in a garage waiting room.

We specialise in SMART repairs (Small to Medium Area Repair Technology), which means we fix the damaged area only — not the whole panel. This keeps costs down and gets you back on the road the same day.

Every repair is backed by our 12-month guarantee. We use professional SMART techniques and OEM-quality materials — the same standard as a main dealer, at a fraction of the price.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a repair take?
Most repairs take between 1 and 3 hours, depending on the type and size of the damage. Alloy wheel repairs are typically done within a couple of hours. You're welcome to carry on with your day while we work.
How much does it cost?
Every job is different, but most of our repairs cost less than a typical insurance excess. We'll always give you an honest, no-obligation quote before we start any work. Call us on 07776 237952 or send photos via WhatsApp for a quick estimate.
Will the colour match my car?
Yes. We use your vehicle's paint code to mix an exact colour match. Once the repair is finished and cured, the repaired area blends seamlessly with the surrounding paintwork.
Do I need to tell my insurance company?
No. Because our repairs cost less than most insurance excesses, the majority of our customers pay directly and avoid any impact on their no-claims bonus. There's no need to make a claim.
What do you need to carry out the repair?
We just need access to a standard 3-pin plug socket and enough space to work around the damaged area of your vehicle. We bring everything else in our fully equipped van.
Do you repair leased or company cars?
Absolutely. We regularly repair lease vehicles before they're returned, helping drivers avoid costly end-of-lease damage charges. A repair with us will almost always be cheaper than the penalty from the leasing company.
