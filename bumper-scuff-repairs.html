About This Service

Mobile Bumper
Scuff Repair

Bumper scuffs are one of the most common types of car damage — tight car parks, low walls, and awkward driveways are responsible for most of the bumper repairs we carry out. The good news is that bumper scuff repair is one of the quickest and most affordable SMART repairs we do.

We come to your home or workplace and carry out the repair on the spot. In most cases your bumper will look as good as new within a few hours, and you'll drive away the same day.

What We Can Repair

  • Light surface scuffs and paint transfer
  • Deeper scuffs requiring filler and repaint
  • Corner damage and scrapes
  • Multi-colour or two-tone bumpers
  • Front and rear bumpers on all makes and models

Our Process

We use SMART repair techniques — Small to Medium Area Repair Technology. We clean and prepare the damaged area, apply filler where needed, repaint using your vehicle's exact colour code, and finish with lacquer and polishing. The result blends seamlessly with the surrounding paintwork.
