About This Service

Mobile Scratch
Repair Service

We cover all types of car scratch — from light surface marks you can barely feel, to deeper key scratches that cut right through to the primer. Whatever the cause, we come to your home or workplace and carry out the repair on the spot.

We use SMART repair techniques: clean and prepare the damaged area, fill any deeper damage, apply OEM paint matched to your vehicle's exact colour code, and finish with a clear coat and careful polish so the repair blends seamlessly with the surrounding paintwork.

Types of Scratch We Repair

  • Light surface scratches — marks on the clear coat caused by bushes, poor washing technique or minor contact. The quickest and most affordable to repair.
  • Key scratches — vandal damage that cuts through to the primer. We fill, repaint and blend for an invisible result.
  • Deep paint scratches — scratches you can feel with your fingernail. Require careful filling, repainting and blending.
  • Stone chips — common on bonnets and front bumpers from motorway driving. Left untreated these can rust.
  • Car park scuffs and paint transfer — scrapes from other vehicles or obstacles in tight car parks.

Our Process

Every scratch repair follows the same meticulous process: we assess the depth and extent of the damage, clean the area thoroughly, apply filler where required, repaint using your vehicle's manufacturer colour code, and lacquer and polish to a showroom finish. All work carries a 12-month guarantee.
