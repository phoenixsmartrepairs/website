About This Service

Mobile Alloy Wheel
Repair Service

Kerbed alloys are one of the most common types of car cosmetic damage — and one of the most visible. Whether it's a single scuffed wheel or all four, we come to your home or workplace and carry out the repair at your door.

Most single wheel repairs are completed within 2 hours. We sand down the damage, fill where required, repaint, and apply lacquer matched to the original finish — so your alloys look as good as new.

Types of Alloy Damage We Repair

  • Kerb damage — we sand down the scuffed area, apply filler, repaint and lacquer to match the original finish.
  • Scuffs and scratches — surface marks from kerbs, car parks or road debris.
  • Painted alloy wheels — full colour-match repaint on painted alloys of any colour.

What We Cannot Repair

  • Buckled or structurally damaged wheels — these are a safety issue and must be replaced.
  • Diamond-cut or CNC-machined alloys — these require specialist lathe equipment and cannot be repaired to a factory standard via SMART techniques.

Thinking of Selling?

Kerbed alloy wheels can knock hundreds of pounds off your car's value. Getting them repaired before a sale or part-exchange is almost always worthwhile — and at our prices, you'll easily recoup the cost.
