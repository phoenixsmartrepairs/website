Alloy Wheel Repairs

Scratches, kerbing damage, chips & cracks - restored to showroom condition at your location.

Restore Your Alloy Wheels

Kerbed or scratched alloy wheels are one of the most common forms of vehicle damage. Whether it happened by accident or just through everyday use, Our mobile alloy wheel repair service restores your wheels to a showroom-finish without the hassle and expense of a garage visit.

We use professional grade materials and match your existing wheel colour to an exact match, ensuring a perfect finish every time. Our service is available across Rugby, Warwickshire, and the surrounding areas.

Signs Your Wheels Need Repair

  • Visible kerbing or scratches on the rim
  • Chipped or pitted alloy surfaces
  • Flaking or bubbling paint
  • Cracks or bends in the alloy
  • Wheels looking dull or discoloured

Our Alloy Repair Process

  1. Inspection - We assess the damage and confirm repairability.
  2. Preparation - Sanding, cleaning, and filling any damaged areas.
  3. Colour Matching - We match your exact wheel colour using professional mixing systems.
  4. Painting & Lacquering - Multiple coats applied for a durable finish.
  5. Curing & Drying - Results harden to a long-lasting shine.

Common Questions

Can you repair cracked alloys?

We can repair minor cracks and kerb damage. Severe structural cracks may require wheel replacement for safety reasons.

Will it match my other wheels?

Yes - we colour match to your existing wheels for a perfect seamless finish.

Do I need to remove the wheels?

No - we work on the wheels while they're still on the vehicle.
